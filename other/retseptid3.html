Supreme Pizza

  • 120g water
  • 0.4 tbsp yeast
  • Pinch of sugar
  • 172g flour
  • 0.5 tbsp oil
  • 0.4 tsp salt

Supreme Pizza Instructions

  1. Mix water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit until foamy.
  2. Add flour, oil, and salt. Knead into dough.
  3. Let the dough rise for 1 hour.
  4. Roll and top with desired ingredients.
  5. Bake at 200°C (400°F) until crust is golden brown.
